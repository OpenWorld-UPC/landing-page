Our Services

Free Plan for all our users

The Free Plan is the most accessible service for all users (Patients and Doctors). You don't need to pay anything extra to use it and it has the following features.

  • Make Medical Consultations (One per day)
  • See the Consultation Hours

Basic Plan

The Basic Plan provides extra features for patients to enhance their experience on the platform

  • See the list of doctors available at that time
  • Review the doctors after a medical consultation

Premium Plan

The Premium Plan offers tools that provide a more satisfying and faster experience for users

  • You can make several medical consultations a day
  • Video call option to directly contact doctors (or patients)
