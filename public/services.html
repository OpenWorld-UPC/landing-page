Our Services

Free Plan

The Free Plan is the most accessible service for all users (Patients and Doctors). You don't need to pay anything extra to use it and it has the following features

  • Both users will have the option of being able to make Medical Consultations, but only once a day.
  • Both users will have access to the Consultation Hours option, where they can consult and choose the schedule.
  • Both users will have the option of having video calls to make their medical consultations.

Price: FREE

Patient Plan

The Patient Plan offers unique features that help provide a better experience when using the platform for our users who are patients

  • All Free Plan features are included. The only difference is the number of Medical Consultations: now you can do it as many times as you want.
  • An exclusive function has been added for patients to be able to book Medical Consultations with a certain specific doctor.
  • An exclusive function has been added for patients to be able to check which doctors are active when they enter the platform.

Price: $3.99

Doctor Plan

The Doctor Plan offers unique functions for doctors to facilitate the use of the platform and adapt it in such a way as to feel comfortable using it

  • All Free Plan features are included. The only difference is the number of Medical Consultations: now you can do it as many times as you want.
  • An exclusive function for doctors so that they can schedule their schedules (Current or Future) depending on their time and their availability at the moment.
  • An exclusive function for doctors that allows them to see the data of the patients with whom they have reserved a consultation.
  • An exclusive function for doctors that allows them to send messages or emails to patients to notify them of anything.

Price: $4.99
